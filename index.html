The Location

On the Renvyle Peninsula, situated on the untamed Wild Atlantic Way passing through Connemara, an oasis of true luxury can be found. Dolphin’s Cove, a resplendent, colourful, thatched roof cottage, fully modernised, sits high above the Atlantic Ocean, with three beaches within a 5 minute drive. With sea views to die for, opulent furnishings, and a host of activities nearby, Dolphin’s Cove is assured to satisfy your whole party, whether they desire adventure or relaxation. With mountains to the rear and ocean to the front, one can’t help feeling in touch with nature.

Classy & Contemporary

Furnishings

But whilst the outside of the house certainly has the appearances of a rustic cottage, the interior is anything but. An open floor plan, elevated by the double-ceiling in the main living room, along with the skylights, breathes life into the cottage and creates a spacious feeling. Equipped with two gas fireplaces, central heating, and a vintage 4 oven Aga stove, even the worst that the Irish winters have to offer will be irrelevant.

What's Inside

Amenities

Georgian windows in the front of the house showcase a cinematic view of the Atlantic, the perfect entertainment companion to the 6 4K televisions, each with an Apple TV and half with Sky TV, with each comfortable bedroom boasting a TV. Each bedroom has feather and down pillows, 5 Star Hotel quality mattresses, 800 thread count Egyptian cotton linens, and numerous blankets and throw pillows. Additionally, an Apple HomePod and trio of Amazon Echo devices make listening the music or checking the news as easy as giving an order! Bookshelves stacked with a range of literature allow for a quieter form of entertainment if that is what you seek! A cocktail set and plenty of chef quality cooking equipment ensure that no matter what you decide to imbibe or dine upon, your cooking process will be enjoyable and easy!
